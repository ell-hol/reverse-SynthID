SynthID Bypass browser demo

A client-side port of the comfy_bypass.py pipeline (itself drawn from the ComfyUI Synthid-Bypass flow). Everything runs in your browser — the image is never uploaded.

What this does: edge-preserving structure lock + phase-randomized high-frequency redraw (high fidelity), with an optional targeted carrier-phase scramble at the documented SynthID bins (stronger, lower fidelity on content-rich images).

Research / AI-safety demo only. This targets a reimplementation of the detector, not Google's actual SynthID verifier. A clean result here is not proof an image is watermark-free.
